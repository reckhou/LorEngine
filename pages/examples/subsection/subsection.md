## Overview

This page is the root of the `subsection/` folder. It appears as an **independent top-level
node** in the tree sidebar — not as a child of Examples — because section roots are always
promoted to the top level.

Its children (`child-one.md`, `child-two.md`) appear nested beneath it when expanded.

## When to use nested sections

Use a subfolder with a matching root file when a topic has enough sub-pages to warrant
grouping — typically three or more related pages that share a parent concept.
